About Drew

drew herald is a photographer based in lexington, kentucky. his work seeks to showcase the intense feelings that come with travel and cultural phenomana. a four month stint in europe as well as shots from numerous locations across america have given herald the opportunity to convey the experiences in life that make it worth living.
