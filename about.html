About Drew

Drew Herald has been shooting film since early 2020, when he recieved an old film camera from his father. Throughout the pandemic he learned the ins and outs of film photogrpahy and development, processing and scanning his own negatives. After a four-month stint in Milan, Italy in early 2022, Drew is based in Lexington, KY where he continues his work.
